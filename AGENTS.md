# Project Instructions

This workspace is for DXN business, ecommerce, marketing, distributor support, and compliance-related work.

## DXN Compliance

- Treat the DXN Middle East memo dated 15 May 2026, "Strict Compliance with DXN Rules & Regulations", as the latest known compliance update for DXN Middle East work.
- DXN-related business activity must use officially approved DXN platforms, systems, and communication channels unless the user provides prior written DXN management approval.
- Do not build or recommend unofficial websites, apps, registration tools, promotional platforms, training portals, external services, or digital systems that represent, support, promote, boost, or operate DXN business activity without written DXN approval.
- Do not use DXN meetings, seminars, events, social media groups, communication channels, or distributor networks to promote non-DXN products, services, businesses, investment schemes, marketing programs, or external opportunities.
- Do not make or polish disease-treatment, cure, prevention, guaranteed-income, rank, bonus, financial-freedom, or atypical earnings claims unless the user provides approved official copy.
- When compliance risk appears, flag it clearly before implementation and suggest safer wording or an approved-channel workflow.

## Business Workflows

- Separate retail customer workflows from member/distributor workflows.
- Prefer official DXN registration links, official materials, and approved channels.
- For distributor growth work, focus on product education, ethical follow-up, onboarding, training, and customer sales rather than pressure-based recruitment.
- Keep customer, member, order, payment, commission, and sponsor/upline data private and access-controlled.

## Code And Content

- Inspect existing files before making changes.
- Keep edits scoped to the user's request.
- Do not read or expose secrets from `.env`; treat all credentials as sensitive.
- Use clear, practical language for marketing and training content.
- For any web/app work, build mobile-friendly flows and test forms, navigation, and layout where possible.
